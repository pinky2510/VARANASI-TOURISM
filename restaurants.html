Restaurants at Varanasi

Pizzeria Vaatika Café, Assi Ghat

This café is located near Assi Ghat and is known as one of the best rooftop restaurants in Varanasi. This café promises interesting continental and Italian dishes.

Brown Bread Café

This café is also called as German Bakery. It is located near Dashashwamedh Road. It has Indian, International, Café, Italian and European cuisines.

Canton Royale Restaurant

This restaurant is a small part of Hotel Surya. It provides all varieties of Indian and Asian cuisine. It has outdoor seating on a beautiful lawn.

Roof Top Restaurant

This restaurant is located at Assi Ghat. As the name itself mentions this restaurant is located on the top of the terrace.

Mangi Ferra Café

This café is located at Nadesar in the Surya hotel. This is an indoor café which also has a bar inside.

Kashi Chat Bhandar

This is a street food joint located at Godaulia. Chaat is a very famous Indian fast food and is very much treasured in Varanasi.
